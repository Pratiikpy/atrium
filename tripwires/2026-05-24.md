## Process note

These 8 tripwires should have been written incrementally across the past
6 weeks as each cut happened. They weren't because the team optimised for
shipping over honesty during the autonomous push. The single biggest
process fix is to **write the tripwire on the same commit that makes the
cut visible**, never later than the next merge.

The format is intentionally minimal: 3 sentences. Stat, action, check-in.
No defending, no excuses, no marketing. Just truth plus next step.
