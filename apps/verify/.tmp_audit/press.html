Brand assets, boilerplate copy, and contact information for journalists and content creators covering Atrium.

Boilerplate

Atrium is a cross-venue portfolio margin protocol on Arbitrum. One wallet posts collateral once and trades across multiple on-chain venues with a single margin number. The protocol uses SPAN-style risk scenarios, formal verification via Kani, and a 48-hour timelocked multisig for upgrades. Currently deployed on Arbitrum Sepolia testnet.

Logo guidelines

  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the “A” glyph around the logo.
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo.
  • Use the dark variant on light backgrounds and the light variant on dark backgrounds.
  • Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 10mm for print.
