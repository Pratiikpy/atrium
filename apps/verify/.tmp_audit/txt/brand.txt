Voice Atrium speaks like a financial-grade product, not a crypto-native one. Specific over poetic, declarative over aspirational, plain over branded. We are Precise. Restrained. Architectural. Quietly confident. We are not Hyped. Memed. Decorative. Approximate. “Revolutionary.” ✓ Do “Atrium computes a SPAN-style cross-product margin number across seven onchain venues.” Specific. Names the mechanism. Counts the venues. Tells you what it is. ✗ Don’t “Atrium revolutionises onchain capital efficiency with next-generation prime brokerage infra.” Marketing slop. Vague. Borrowed from every other DeFi pitch deck. VI . Trademark and usage What you can and cannot do with the Atrium name and assets. You may Refer to Atrium by name in editorial, journalistic, or research contexts Embed the wordmark in articles or research notes that reference Atrium Build venue adapters and reference the Atrium name in your project Use the colour palette and typography for derivative open-source work You may not Use the Atrium name or wordmark to imply endorsement of a product Distribute a competing product under the Atrium name Alter the wordmark’s letterforms, italic angle, or tracking Use the wordmark on collateral that misrepresents Atrium’s offering VII .
